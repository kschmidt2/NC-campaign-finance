Outside influence

By Kiersten Schmidt

North Carolina Governor Pat McCrory and challenger Roy Cooper are locked in a tight race for the governor's mansion heading into Tuesday's election. Both racked up millions in donations from July through October as the race headed down to the wire. With the race receiving national attention in light of the media coverage of HB2, donors in other states have contributed to both campaigns. These donations made up about 10 percent of the total given to McCrory and Cooper. Where are these donations coming from?
